About Rock View

Rock View Productions is a freelance audio engineering company based out of a studio in Wheat Ridge, with access to a professional studio as well as a home/project studio. Both studios have access to Pro Tools and the newest version of Cubase. We use name brand plug ins and gear that is used very often in the audio industry. I have experience with almost any type of genre of music from punk rock, to jazz, to hip hop.
